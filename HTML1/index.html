masterpieces

> Among Christopher Nolan’s greatest masterpieces stand *Interstellar*, *Inception*, and *The Dark Knight* — each redefining modern cinema in its own way. *Interstellar* reaches beyond space and time to explore love, sacrifice, and the essence of human survival, turning science into pure poetry. *Inception* dives into the architecture of dreams, blending emotion and intellect in a multilayered journey through the human subconscious. And with *The Dark Knight*, Nolan transforms the superhero genre into a moral and psychliogical epic, where chaos and order clilide in a haunting reflection of humanity.
